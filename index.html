Who I Am

I am final-year Mechanical Engineering student at Roorkee Institute of Technology with a deep-seated passion for transforming innovative ideas into tangible realities. My journey in engineering is driven by a relentless curiosity and an eagerness to learn and adapt. I have honed my hands-on experience through various projects involving CAD, SolidWorks, and Fusion 360, complemented by my programming skills in C++ and my work with an embedded systems like Arduino and Raspberry Pi. I thrive in collaborative environments and am recognized for my punctuality, hard work, and team-player spirit. Beyond engineering, I find joy in creative expression through poetry and singing, and I am always exploring the fascinating world of Artificial Intelligence.
